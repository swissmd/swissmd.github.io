Why is the Pocket Truth Teller shaped the way it is?

The unique shape of the Pocket Turth Teller makes its responses very easy to read. The design came in a vision that incorporates sacred geometry: it combines two different sized pyramids, one based on the dimensions of the Great Pyramid of Giza, the other is a geometric pyramid based on equilateral triangles.

What is it made of

The Pocket Truth Teller is made of solid brass (an alloy of zinc and copper, which is highly conductive to electromagnetic energy). The Pocket Truth Teller is also gold plated to prevent the tarnishing of the brass.

What is the size

The Pocket Truth Teller weighs 14 grams (1/2 ounce) which makes it very responsive and ideal for all purpose dowsing. It measures about 1 inch from top to bottom and is about .5 inches in width.

How long is the chain

The chain is about 8 inches long (20 centimeters), which makes it very flexible, especially for those who find it easier to receive answers with a longer chain. You can adjust the chain to any length.

How easy is it to use

We include very simple illustrated instructions so you can learn to use it in just a few moments. Therafter it will answer your questions immediately.

How do you ship

At the moment there is no express shipping but most orders will get to their destination in 7 to 10 days (mostly via UPS - with tracking). The Free shipping offer is valid only for the USA. We reserve the right to ask for supplementary shipping depending on International destinations.
